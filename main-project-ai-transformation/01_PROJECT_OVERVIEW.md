## The Business Problem

Spectrum.Life is growing at 60% year-on-year. In 2023, interactions with employee mental health services increased 9x compared to 2022. The platform now serves 7.5 million individuals across 4,000+ corporate clients and 50+ universities in Ireland and the UK.

That growth is the problem.

Every new member who accesses support triggers a manual chain: intake call, triage assessment, risk evaluation, pathway decision, counsellor matching, session booking, outcome tracking, case closure. Each step handled by a human. Each step taking time that compounds across millions of interactions.

The clinical and operational teams that built Spectrum.Life's reputation for quality were designed for a different scale. The processes, tools, and workflows have not kept pace with the growth. AI transformation is not a nice-to-have — it is a business continuity requirement.

---

## Vision

> Every person who contacts Spectrum.Life gets the right support, at the right level, at the right speed — consistently, safely, and at scale.

AI removes the manual bottlenecks that slow clinical delivery, create inconsistency, and consume coordinator time that should be spent on people, not process.

## Programme Scope

Two parallel workstreams running simultaneously:

### Workstream 1 — Salesforce & Agentforce Automation
Automating operational and clinical support processes using Salesforce flows, process automation, and Agentforce AI agents.

**In scope:** Intake coordination · Case routing · Referral management · Clinical outcome tracking · HR and people operations · Finance and billing workflows · Reporting and dashboard automation

**Out of scope (Phase 1):** Direct clinical decision-making · Patient-facing chatbot deployment · Full EHR integration

### Workstream 2 — Claude & AI Model Development
Building intelligent AI solutions using Claude (Anthropic) — embedded into clinical, operational, and administrative workflows.

**In scope:** Clinical triage assistance · Session documentation · Referral letter generation · Outcome measurement · Manager guidance tools · Prompt library development · AI governance framework

**Out of scope (Phase 1):** Autonomous clinical decisions · Unmonitored AI-to-patient interaction · External API integrations beyond Salesforce

## Guiding Principles

These principles govern every decision made in this programme:

**1. Human-in-the-loop is non-negotiable**
No AI output in a clinical context is acted upon without human review. AI assists; clinicians decide.

**2. Governance before deployment**
Every AI workflow is reviewed against GDPR Article 9 and the EU AI Act before going live. No exceptions.

**3. BA requirements layer, not technical implementation**
The AI Transformation Partner role defines what the business needs and translates it into precise specifications. Development and configuration are delivered by platform partners and technical teams.

**4. Start with the highest-pain processes**
Automation is prioritised by impact — time saved, risk reduced, consistency gained. Not by what is technically easiest.

**5. Build to scale, not to demo**
Every solution is designed with Spectrum.Life's 7.5 million member base in mind. Pilots are validated before rollout. Rollouts are monitored before scaling.
