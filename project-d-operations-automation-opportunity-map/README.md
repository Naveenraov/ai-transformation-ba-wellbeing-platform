## The Prioritisation Logic

Every opportunity was assessed on two axes:

**Automation Potential** — volume, repetition, data readiness, current pain level

**Feasibility** — risk level, technical dependency, change readiness

| Tier | Label | Criteria |
|------|-------|----------|
| 🔴 Quick Win | Start now | High impact + low effort + existing tools |
| 🟠 Strategic Build | Plan and build | High impact + medium effort + some integration |
| 🟡 Future State | Sequence later | High impact + higher effort + Agentforce or new pipelines |
| ⚪ Low Priority | Revisit later | Lower volume or lower impact |

## The 3 Quick Wins

### Quick Win 1 — Clinical Inbox Triage & Case Note Generator
**Tool:** Claude API | **Timeline:** Weeks 1–3

Case Managers receive 30-50 messages per day across email, WhatsApp, and chat. Every message is read and actioned manually. Claude classifies by urgency and type, drafts suggested responses, and generates structured audit-ready case notes from a 2-sentence summary.

**Success metric:** 35–45 minutes of admin time saved per Case Manager per day within 30 days.

---

### Quick Win 2 — HR FAQ Chatbot Expansion
**Tool:** Claude Projects | **Timeline:** Weeks 2–4

One live Claude deployment already exists at Spectrum.Life — the MS Teams HR FAQ chatbot. This quick win expands it into a full Claude Project with persistent context covering onboarding, benefits, leave, and CPD queries — and adds personalised onboarding document pack generation for each new hire.

**Success metric:** HR query volume handled by Claude increases from ~20% to 60%+ within 60 days.

---

### Quick Win 3 — Sales Renewal Risk Scoring & Proposal Drafting
**Tool:** Salesforce Flow + Claude | **Timeline:** Weeks 3–6

With 3,000 corporate clients, renewal management is a direct revenue protection play. Salesforce Flow calculates a renewal risk score per account from usage data, NPS, and contract end date — flagging at-risk accounts automatically. Claude generates first-draft renewal proposals from account data.

**Success metric:** At-risk account identification lead time increased by 60+ days. Proposal time reduced from 2–3 hours to 30 minutes.

## Governance Principles Applied

Every automation in this map is governed by four principles:

- **Clinical boundary** — Claude handles admin only. Clinical decisions stay with qualified clinicians. Human-in-the-loop at every clinical step.
- **GDPR Article 9** — Special category clinical data handled with full consent mapping and DPAs.
- **EU AI Act alignment** — Internal tools assessed against same high-risk criteria applied to Cara (Spectrum.Life's CE-certified AI companion).
- **Audit trail** — Every Claude-generated output reviewed and approved by a human before action. All approvals logged.
